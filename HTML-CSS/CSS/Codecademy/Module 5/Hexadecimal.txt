A hex color begins with a hash character (#) which is followed 
by three or six characters. The characters represent values 
for red, blue and green.

darkseagreen: #8FBC8F
sienna:       #A0522D
saddlebrown:  #8B4513
brown:        #A52A2A
black:        #000000 or #000
white:        #FFFFFF or #FFF
aqua:         #00FFFF or #0FF

You can include hex colors just as you would include named colors: 
background-color: #9932cc;, and the letters can be uppercase or 
lowercase.
