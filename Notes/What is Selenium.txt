What is Selenium?

Selenium is a free (open source) automated testing suite for web applications accross different browsers/platforms.
Four Main Components: Selenium (IDE), Selenium (Remote Control), WebDriver, Selenium Grid
Selenium 1 refers to Selenium (RC)
Selenium 2 is a combination of Selenium (RC) & WebDriver


Who Developed Selenium?

The suite was designed by Jason Huggins but the tools within the suite were created by separate devs.
