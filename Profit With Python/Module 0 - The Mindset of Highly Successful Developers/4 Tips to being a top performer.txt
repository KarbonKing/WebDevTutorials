#4 Tips to being a top performer

1. Always Give Value First
        - Come from a mindset of "How can I provide value to those I rely on?"

2. Never say you can't do something
        - Come from the mindset of "How can I do it?"
        - Go out of your way for people, unless they're A-holes..

3. Take full responsibility for your FAILURE and SUCCESS
        - Recognize all things within your power and see them through to completion

4. Be Brutally honest with yourself
        - Be honest about your work and it's quality.
        - PDA: Public Display of Accountability (Share your progress with others and keep yourself accountable)
        - If you don't know something, say something. Don't play the genius..
